Fields Where Women Are Lacking Empowerment

Despite progress in many areas, women still face significant challenges and underrepresentation in several key fields. Addressing these gaps is essential for achieving gender equality and leveraging the full potential of women’s contributions:

Addressing these disparities requires dedicated efforts to create opportunities, provide support, and remove barriers for women in these fields. Signature Sisters is committed to driving progress and empowering women to excel and lead in every domain.

Technology and STEM Underrepresentation:

Women make up only 28% of the workforce in STEM fields (UNESCO). This gap is evident in both educational attainment and professional roles, where women are often underrepresented and face barriers to entry and advancement.

Leadership and Executive Roles Corporate Leadership:

Women hold just 24% of senior leadership positions in companies globally (McKinsey). This disparity reflects a broader issue of gender inequality in decision-making roles and organizational leadership.

Entrepreneurship and Business Ownership Funding and Support:

Female entrepreneurs receive only 2% of venture capital funding worldwide (Crunchbase). Women often encounter difficulties in accessing funding, resources, and networks necessary to start and scale businesses.

Political Representation Legislative Participation:

Women occupy only 26% of parliamentary seats around the world (Inter-Parliamentary Union). Their limited presence in political decision-making bodies hinders their ability to influence policies and drive systemic change.
